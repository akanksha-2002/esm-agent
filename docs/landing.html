Momentra runs your ESM psychology study end-to-end — scheduling prompts, tracking compliance, adapting questions, and discovering hypotheses from your live data automatically.

Chat directly with your MongoDB data. Ask "why is boredom high on Tuesdays?" and get an answer grounded in real numbers.

How it works

1

Design your study

Tell the Study Designer Agent your research question. It generates a complete ESM protocol — questions, sampling windows, duration, and confound controls.

2

Participants respond in real time

Participants fill a lightweight form at random times throughout the day. Every response lands in MongoDB Atlas instantly via a Cloud Function webhook.

3

Agents reason about the data

After every submission, Gemini 2.5 Flash analyzes the full dataset, generates adaptive follow-up questions for high-boredom responses, and updates the researcher dashboard.

4

Discover what you didn't know to look for

The Statistician Agent surfaces hypotheses like "Work/studying shows paradoxically high focus and high boredom simultaneously" — patterns you'd miss in a spreadsheet.
